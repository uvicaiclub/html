The UVic AI Club is the cornerstone of artificial intelligence interest at the University of Victoria. Our mandate is to foster interest in AI research and to advocate for university programs which lead to degrees in AI disciplines.

We also participate in AI based competitions, theorize about practical AI solutions, and offer guidance for individuals interested in learning and improving their skills in AI programming.

The primary technique we study is machine learning algorithms. These tools allow us to train a computer quickly. Neural networks are broken down and analyzed for effective tool use. We can then analyze current data against past data to provide a lightning fast response.
